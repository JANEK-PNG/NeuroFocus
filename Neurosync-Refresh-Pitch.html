v Diagnosis · typography & surfaces

"Looks like 2010."

font-family: 'JetBrains Mono' — 25+ occurrences
Monospace as decoration = core nerd signal. Datadog / Linear / Vercel 2018-era. Labels, time display, metrics — everywhere.
Uppercase tiny labels + heavy letter-spacing (.22em)
Bootstrap-era pattern. Reads like a backend admin tool.
Dark elevated cards #131826 over #0A0E1A, rgba-white borders 0.06, inset highlights
Glassmorphism residue. Apple Music 2014 / iOS 7. Modern apps are flat or organically gradient.
Neon glow halos via box-shadow rgba(...,0.4)
Cyberpunk era residue. 2025 wellness apps use diffused light, not neon.

vi Diagnosis · metaphor & density

A medical device.

The deepest issue isn't paint — it's that the UI behaves like a scientific instrument. Five metrics on top. Frequency in hertz. A Windows-style audio mixer. A literal brain logo.

Visible jargon: "10 HZ ALPHA", "ENTRAINED", δ/θ/α/β/γ
Operating-a-medical-device vibe. The science should be hidden in settings, not headlining the timer.
Top strip: 5 metrics + 3 presets + theme toggle + brain logo
Info-dump. Modern category shows one thing at a time.
Windows-style volume mixer with per-channel sliders
Workstation aesthetic. DJ console, not a focus timer.
Footer: "binaural beats · all data stored locally"
Privacy-nerd badge. Reassures developers, alienates everyone else.

viii Direction

Six moves the leaders share.

i
Light-default, warm off-white.
Dark mode becomes opt-in. Cream / paper / dawn — never near-black.
ii
Soft chromatic gradient washes.
Pastel, drifting, atmospheric. No neon, no flat dark, no glow halos.
iii
One sans, optionally one humanist serif.
Drop all monospace. Manrope alone is fine. Mono → developer-tool tell.
iv
Big calm numerals, almost no labels.
Time + one verb. Hide bands, hertz, entrainment behind a settings sheet.
v
One visible action.
Progressive disclosure. The mixer, journeys, bands collapse into a drawer.
vi
Organic motion.
Breath cycles, drifting gradients. No spinning rings, no neon pulse.

ix Three paths

Same brief, three depths.

A
Visual refresh.
Keep the ring, mixer, brain-dump, and journey selector. Re-skin every surface. Light warm bg. Drop mono. Hide Hz. Soft gradient backdrop. Organic motion.
lowest risk ~1 day
B
One calm screen.
Restructure to the Headspace / One Sec model. Time + play, front and center. Mixer, bands, journey, brain-dump collapse behind one drawer.
recommended ~3 days
C
New metaphor.
Rethink the ring entirely. Could be a drifting orb, a breath-circle, an aurora field, a light gradient that thickens over the session. Bigger swing, bigger upside.
biggest swing ~1 week
