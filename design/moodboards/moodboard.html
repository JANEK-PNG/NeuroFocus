Chapter I

The Brief

From a futuristic lab to a place a person actually wants to sit.

Board feedback (verbatim)

"The UI looks like it's for futuristic tech people, not for the people who use brainwaves as a way of working. The design needs to connect more to nature, earthy colors, less computer-like vibes."

What that means in design language

Drop the navy-and-cyan dashboard energy. Replace it with surfaces that feel held, not operated. Bring in earthy colors, a serif voice, subtle paper grain, slower motion. Keep every feature; only the skin and the tone change.

Three concepts on offer

  • Forest Floor — earthy, woodsy, golden hour
  • Stone & Tide — quiet, ceramic, contemplative
  • Dawn Garden — warm, hopeful, morning routine

Constraints

Single-file HTML/CSS/JS app. No new dependencies. All features retained: Pomodoro presets, focus journeys, timer, entrainment engine (5 bands), ambient mixer, internet radio, brain dump. Light + dark themes both required. Light mode is the hero for all three concepts — a deliberate shift from the current dark-first identity.

Decision

Common ground (all three agree)

  • Kill JetBrains Mono. Tabular serif numerals instead. Drops 80% of the "tech" feel in one move.
  • Add a serif — Fraunces is the consensus pick (variable, optical sizes, ink-trap warmth).
  • Drop cyan & navy completely. All palettes are warm-paper light + deep-earth dark.
  • Subtle SVG noise @ ~4% opacity, multiply-blended.
  • Softer microcopy. Fewer imperatives, more invitations.
  • Slower motion. 480–680ms eased exhales, zero bounces or springs.

Studio recommendation

Stone & Tide. Defensible identity ("the stone app"), lower risk than Forest's blob, more distinctive than Dawn's flower. Variable typography ties beautifully into the entrainment narrative — as the mind softens, so do the digits.

Pull microcopy + texture warmth from Dawn Garden; pull "begin sitting" tone from Forest Floor where it fits. Best of all three lives in the chosen frame.

Board decision needed

  1. Pick a concept — ABCHybrid
  2. Approve font swap — Fraunces + Inter, drop JetBrains Mono
  3. Approve "bold move" — blob / stone / flower — or defer to v2
  4. Approve microcopy direction — quieter, more invitational
